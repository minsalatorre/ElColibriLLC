About us

Back in 2022...

El Colibri was established with a passion for sharing the vibrant flavors and traditional recipes from Mexico.

For us the decision to emphasize more plants based meals is a way to reconnect with our Indigenous roots. While fish and wild game were a part of the pre-Hispanic diet, plants formed the foundation of our Indigenous food culture.

Our goal here at El Colibri is simple; we strive to serve our customers real Mexico City style street food that is consistently delicious and always affordable.
